Welcome to Fl4g Sm4sher

Fl4g Sm4sher is a group of college students from NIT Durgapur who are passionate about cyber security and dedicated to protecting tomorrow's cyberspace. Our team of mentors trains freshers in cyber security through CTFs and we organize infosec events, trainings, and work on a wide variety of cyber security research projects.
